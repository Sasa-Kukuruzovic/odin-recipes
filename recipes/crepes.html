Crepes Recipe

Description

CrÃªpes are very thin pancakes. They can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings. The breakfast staple dates back to at least 13th-century France.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, eggs, milk, melted butter, salt, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  3. Pour a small amount of batter into the skillet and swirl to coat the bottom evenly.
  4. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until the edges start to lift, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Repeat with the remaining batter, stacking the cooked crepes on a plate.
  6. Serve warm with your choice of fillings and toppings, such as Nutella, fruit, or whipped cream.
